How?

Saxo_Broko uses Ecologi and Treedom to offset our carbon footprint. Ecologi alone offsets the total carbon emissions by more than 4x per year, with 20T of emissions being offset. Ecologi also helps local economies. Treedom offsets 38.5kg of carbon per year and helps the local economies. Trees can be donated through either Treedom or Ecologi, but Ecologi is preferred.
